The cab is one of the favourite public transport options for getting between places in cities. It is extremely comfortable, flexible, do not require walking to a metro station or a bus stop, and send you directly to your location of choice. It is expensive however. CabShare Finder proposes to reduce the cost by connecting users who are looking for cabs in the same area to share a trip in the same direction.

Alice is shopping in Ngee Ann City, Orchard. She will be finished around 4 p.m. and planning to take the cab home to her flat in Clementi. With CabShare Finder app, she can share this intention, even as she is shopping. Now Bob is also in the Orchard Area. Tired as he is, he is considering whether to take the crowded trip home, or hire a cab. Using the app, he finds that he can halve his usual cab fare by sharing the trip with Alice - a nice bargain.
